Health Promotion/Disease Prevention HPDP4

To assess a patient clinically, in OPD and elsewhere, in particular to:

TITLEPREVENTION OF EYE INJURY

Code

HPDP4

Learning Outcome

  • Use appropriate eye protection with ophthalmic lasers.
  • Promote the use of resources and information regarding eye injury prevention.

Assessment

CbD Part 2 FRCOphth 

Assessor

Target Year of Achievement

Year 7

Related Learning Outcomes

Overview
  • Be aware of what precautions should be advised for workers in chemical/glass manufacture.
  • Recognise your responsibilities regarding safety in the workplace – for fellow staff and for patients – and what precautions should be taken in this regard (environment, condition of equipment etc.).
  • Be aware of occupations, leisure activities and criminal activities associated with ophthalmic injuries.
  • Understand what eye protection is available and how to use it.
  • Always apply principles and practice of risk management and patient safety.
